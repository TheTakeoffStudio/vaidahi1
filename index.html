Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer customised stitching?

Yes — share your child's measurements at checkout and we'll tailor the outfit to fit beautifully.

How long does delivery take?

Ready-to-ship pieces arrive in 3–5 business days. Customised orders take 7–10 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer 7-day easy returns on non-customised items. See our Return Policy for details.

Which fabrics do you use?

Premium cotton blends, tulle, satin and hand-embroidered fabrics — all chosen for sensitive young skin.
